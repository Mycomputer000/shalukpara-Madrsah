About Shalukpara Madrasah Ashraful Uloom

A BREIF INTRODUCTION

The Shalukpara madrasah Ashraful uloom is one of the most prestigiuos and historical educational institution which teaches the deen(religious education). Since 1970 it is enrriching students from different districts and states with both tradition and religious education.Many teachers are the alumni of prestigious darul uloom deoband who are striving to educate and enrich the students with knowledge. This institution is serving the purpose of eradicating alll the shirk and follies in the name of islam form the society.Sometimes it provides seminar on some humanitarian issues like health consciuosness.For now it has 23 branches in howrah and its success is the outcome of goodwill and dua.We have been able to begin the full course of maulana with the mercy of Allah.The course for 2018-2019 will begin soon.
